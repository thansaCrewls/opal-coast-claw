Barusan saya selesai buatkan website untuk **Opal Coast Claw** - nail salon specialist BIAB di Sentul City, Bogor.

Website ini dibuat dengan konsep:
- ✨ **Elegant & Feminine** - warna soft opal, dusty rose, lavender, gold

### 🎯 Fitur Website

**7 Sections Lengkap:**
1. **Hero Section** - Full-screen gradient dengan CTA Book Now langsung ke WhatsApp
2. **About** - Cerita tentang studio boutique dan keunggulan BIAB
3. **Services** - Card-based grid dengan focus pada BIAB (Builder In A Bottle)
4. **Gallery** - Masonry photo gallery showcasing nail art
5. **Reviews** - Google rating 5.0 ⭐ dengan 3 testimonial
6. **Contact** - WhatsApp CTA, phone, address, embedded Google Maps
7. **Footer** - Links dan social media (Instagram, WhatsApp, Maps)

**Bonus Features:**
- 🎨 Animasi smooth scroll (AOS library)
- 📱 100% responsive - mobile, tablet, desktop
- 💬 WhatsApp floating button (selalu visible di pojok kanan bawah)
- 🗺️ Google Maps embedded
- ⭐ Star rating badge
- 🖼️ Gallery dengan hover effects
