Everything you need to know about BIAB nails

What is BIAB?

BIAB (Builder In A Bottle) is a strong, flexible gel overlay that protects natural nails and helps them grow longer. It lasts 3-4 weeks with minimal chipping.

How long does BIAB last?

Typically 3-4 weeks. We recommend infills every 2-4 weeks to maintain the look and keep your natural nails healthy.

Is BIAB damaging to natural nails?

No! When applied and removed properly, BIAB actually strengthens and protects your natural nails, helping them grow longer and healthier.

What's the difference between BIAB and gel polish?

BIAB is thicker and adds structure/strength to nails. Gel polish is just color. BIAB lasts longer and is better for nail health.

How long does the service take?

BIAB Full Set: 1.5-2 hours. BIAB Infill: 1-1.5 hours. French BIAB: 2-2.5 hours.
