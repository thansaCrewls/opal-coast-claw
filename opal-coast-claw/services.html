What is BIAB?

BIAB (Builder In A Bottle) is a revolutionary gel system that creates a strong, flexible overlay on your natural nails. Unlike regular gel polish, BIAB adds structure and protection, helping your nails grow longer and healthier.

💪 Stronger than gel 🌱 Helps nail growth ⏰ Lasts 3-4 weeks ✨ Minimal chipping
