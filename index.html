About Us

A boutique studio for beautiful nails

Welcome to Opal Coast Claw — where nail artistry meets coastal serenity. Nestled in a private cluster home studio in Sentul City, we specialize in BIAB (Builder In A Bottle) nails, offering a premium, personalized experience away from the bustle of traditional salons.

Our philosophy is simple: healthy nails are beautiful nails. Using professional-grade products and expert techniques, we create strong, flexible overlays that protect your natural nails while delivering a flawless, long-lasting finish that outperforms regular gel manicures.

Every set is crafted with care, tailored to your lifestyle and aesthetic preferences.

Our Services

BIAB Specialist

BIAB (Builder In A Bottle) is a revolutionary gel system that creates a strong, flexible overlay on your natural nails. Unlike regular gel polish, BIAB adds structure and protection, helping your nails grow longer and healthier while lasting 3-4 weeks with minimal chipping.

Why Choose BIAB?

Stronger than regular gel polish
Helps nails grow naturally
Lasts 3-4 weeks
Minimal chipping & lifting
